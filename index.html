Biography

I completed my undergraduate studies at East China University of Science and Technology (华东理工大学), and obtained my Master’s and PhD degrees at Imperial College London under the supervision of Prof. Dmitry Turaev. Before joining SCMS, I held postdoctoral positions at the University of São Paulo, the University of Padova, and Imperial College London.

I used to do serious landscape photography (before Covid-19).
